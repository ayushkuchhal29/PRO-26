                             *RECIPIE OF NOODELS*
 

* boil the noodels for 10-15 minutes in water.
* take a utensil and add oil to utensil and leave for 2 minutes.
* add garlic and ginger paste to utensil and let it cook for 3 minutes.
* now add salt and vegetables like onion capsicum carrots etc and cook it for 3 minutes. 
* now add the soy sauce and noodels which you boiled before.
                    *YOUR NOODELS ARE READY*
